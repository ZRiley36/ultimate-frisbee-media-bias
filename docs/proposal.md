Data 301 Final Project Proposal

We intend to investigate the frequency of news reports on Cal Poly’s men’s club ultimate frisbee team, Slocore, and compare it to the frequency of reports on other teams within their division. We will also juxtapose the frequency of a team's coverage with their national rank, to determine if any team is being disproportionately covered. If particular reporters have biases for or against Cal Poly, we will be able to investigate that bias by grouping by the author of each post. We may discover that keywords associated with Cal Poly’s team are particularly positive or negative. 

Our possible research questions are:

What is the proportion of news reports about Cal Poly's men's club frisbee team, Slocore, in comparison to other men's frisbee teams?

Are there significant differences in the media coverage of Cal Poly's Slocore frisbee team compared to other men's frisbee teams, and if so, what factors contribute to these differences?

Which colleges receive the most news coverage for their men's frisbee teams, and conversely, which colleges receive the least coverage?

What is the relationship between the region and rank of team with the team’s media coverage?

Is there a bias, either positive or negative, in the reporting of Cal Poly's Slocore frisbee team? If so, can this bias be attributed to specific authors or sources?

What are the keywords and phrases frequently associated with Cal Poly's Slocore frisbee team in news reports, and do these keywords have predominantly positive or negative connotations?

We hope to gather news reports, blogs, and posts regarding Men’s D1 college frisbee from the primary news source of Ultimate Frisbee: UltiWorld. Their website houses a plethora of articles on the college division:  https://ultiworld.com/division/usau-college-d-i-mens/. We can scrape this data and search for keywords such as Cal Poly Slocore, players’ names, and opposing teams to get a good sense of any bias in frisbee reporting. 

This topic intrigues us because our peers compete on Slocore, and have often expressed discontent with their lack of converge on Ultiworld. Obviously, this attitude is biased, but other teams have corroborated the belief that Cal Poly’s team is underreported. Are these beliefs the result of group think and confirmation bias, or are there other factors at play?
